Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

BloomCycles ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our reproductive health tracking application.

What Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Date of birth
  • Device information (iOS version, device type)
  • App usage data and preferences

Health Information

  • Menstrual cycle data
  • Fertility tracking information
  • Pregnancy-related data
  • Symptoms and health notes
  • Medication and supplement logs

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized health tracking and insights
  • Improve our AI prediction algorithms
  • Send important notifications and reminders
  • Generate health reports and summaries
  • Enhance app features and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive health data
  • HIPAA-compliant storage and processing
  • Secure authentication with biometric options
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Data backup and disaster recovery protocols

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., sharing with healthcare providers)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist in app operations
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for research purposes

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your health data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
